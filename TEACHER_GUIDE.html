👩‍🏫 How to Generate Your Class Schedule

1

Open the portal URL → Click Teacher Portal

2

Select your Grade and Section

3

Enter your name as Class Teacher (CT) and your co-teacher as Associate CT (ACT)

4

Tick Day 1 and enter the PTM date. Tick Day 2 only if PTM is spread across 2 days.

5

Set Break 1 (Snack — max 20 min) start and end time

6

Set Break 2 (Lunch — max 30 min) start and end time

7

Click Generate My PTM Schedule

📥 Two Downloads Available

Sheet 1

PTM Circular — Give to parents

Sl. No. · Time Slot · Student Name only
No signature column · NAFL official format

Sheet 2

Attendance Sheet — Keep with you

Sl. No. · Time Slot · Student Name · Parent's Signature
Use to collect signatures during PTM

Each sheet is available as PDF, Word, and Print. Excel downloads both sheets together.

🔒 About Sibling Timings

Students with siblings in other classes are marked in bold with a 🔒 lock icon.

Their timings are fixed by Admin with a 20–30 minute gap between siblings so parents can attend both PTMs without missing either. You cannot change these timings.
